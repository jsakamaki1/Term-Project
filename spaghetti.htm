Spaghetti - Recipe

October 10, 2019

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Italian Sausage (I prefer mild)
  • 1 Jar of your favorite red sauce - I typically use Vodka Sauce from Trader Joes when I make this
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 3-4 Cloves of garlic
  • 4 Cups of Baby Spinach (or as much or as little as you like, I just love having a lot of spinach and it cooks down)
  • 1 lb Pasta of your choice
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Boil a pot of water, for the pasta. While waiting for the water to boil take the sausage out of it's casing and brown in a pan over medium heat. Then while it's cooking, dice up the onion. When the sausage is about half way done, add the diced onion to the pan. Cook until onions are translucent and sausage is completely cooked (no pink). Add in pasta and cook according to package guidelines.
  2. Stir in the jar of sauce, let it simmer over low heat for a few minutes. Add in salt and pepper to taste. And slowly add in the spinach, it'll slowly cook down. Simmer on low.
  3. Once pasta is down, strain the pasta and immediately add the pasta to the sauce. Slowly stir together and add the parmesan cheese. Once everything is all nicely mixed, serve and ENJOY!
